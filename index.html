About Mayavi

Māyāvi is a Malayalam comic strip appearing in the Indian comic magazine Balarama.
Māyāvi, the protagonist in the series, is an imp, who helps keep the forest safe from villains and dark wizards.
Most of the stories revolves around how two dark magicians and their companions try to capture Māyāvi, but fail hilariously.
Māyāvi takes place in a secluded forest in Kerala.
